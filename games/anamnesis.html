ANAMNESIS

UE4

at: NYU Game Center (Fall 2021) (team of 4)
role: audio lead | design | programming | narrative

ANAMNESIS is a first-person action-adventure prototype created based on John Eblahan's Brutalist.

The player plays as a serial killer who had lost all his memories, yet still haunted by guilt, and is trying to find some sort of closure for his victims.

This project is developed over 4 weeks as a proof of concept at NYU Game Center.
