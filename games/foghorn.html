Foghorn

Unity 3D

at: NYU Game Center (Spring 2022)
role: everything

Foghorn is the prototype of a multiplayer horror game concept.

It is about a group of survivors shipwrecked on a mysterious island engulfed by fog, and a haunting humanoid figure tracking them down...

This project is developed throughout NYU Game Center's 3D Studio class. Main features include a rough enemy AI that scans for the player. To lose them, break line of sight with the enemy for a short period of time.
