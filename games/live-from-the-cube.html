Live from the Cube

Unity 3D

at: NYU Game Center (Spring 2022) (team of 6)
role: design lead | audio lead | programming | tech art | animations

Live from the Cube is a 5-month project at NYU Game Center. It's a game about juggling between tasks.

You play as Jerry, an average 9-5 office worker who is trying to balance a rhythm game streaming career with his work life.

The game features a procedurally generated narrative through emails, stat managing based on how well he performs in streaming and the times he was caught by the boss.
