HyperStars is an arcade style 2D fighting game with a unique tug of war health mechanic. Its combat puts focus on mobility and positioning in aggressive and hype 1v1 battles.

Inspired by y2k character designs and anime megacities, HyperStars puts style at the heart of its game and visual direction.

Welcome to METROPOLIKA

Metropolika is the name of the futuristic city-state where the game takes place. Metropolika is a sterile, corporate utopia. It is a massive city that splits into eight distinct occupation based districts, known as factions.
