Ritual Night

Under Development

Unity 2D

at: Outfox Games (team of 5)
role: design co-lead | audio lead | programming | website | tech art | media

Ritual Night is a multi-player social-deduction party game with stealth elements.

The players play as a group of thrill-seeking monsters trying to have a good time at a party while completing few rituals to summon the mighty Cthulhu. But watch out! There are humans in disguise among you, and their goal is to sabotage...

Ritual Night is set to release in Early Access in Winter 2022.
