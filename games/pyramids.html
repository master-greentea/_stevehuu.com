PYRAMIDS

2021 Fall

UE4

with: NYU GameCenter
role: everything

Pyramids (working title) is a third-person adventure game prototype set in a bizarre post-apocalypse world. The protagonist wakes up to find themselves in a colorless world where they need to find fragments of the past to piece together what the hell happened. This project is created during NYU Game Center's 3D Studio class. The players will be able to fully explore the broken world and find numerous narrative pieces that may or may not lead them closer to the truth.
