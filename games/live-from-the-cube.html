Live from the Cube

2021-2022 Spring

Unity

with: NYU Game Center (team of 6)
role: design lead | audio lead | programming | tech art | animations

Live from the Cube is a 5 months project at NYU Game Center. It's a game about juggling between tasks. You play as Jerry, a 9-5 office worker who is trying to balance his rhythm game streaming career with his work life.

The game features a procedurally generated narrative through Jerry's emails, a stat managing system through how well he performs in streaming and how many times he gets caught by the boss.
