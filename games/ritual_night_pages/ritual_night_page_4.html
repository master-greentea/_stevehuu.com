Death of the Moon:
A Ritual Night Story

To fully expand the lore and serve as a form of retention for the players, I wrote a 34-page-story that both expands on existing lore and creates suspense for future updates.

The story is broken down into chapters that can be collected in-game.

Collection menu for the story

Here's an excerpt:

I grew up in rural New England, a place where strange tales were commonplace. Stories of mysterious creatures, people vanishing near the sea, and whispered accounts of cult gatherings were as much a part of my childhood as the crashing waves. A part of me has always believed in these tales. I have seen the look of true terror in the eyes of fishermen recounting their stories. That same look now burned in the eyes of the man standing before me. And I couldn't look away.
