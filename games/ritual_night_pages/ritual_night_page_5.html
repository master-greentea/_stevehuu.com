Theme Songs

In the main theme of Ritual Night (Elder Gods in Party Hats), I made use of many leitmotifs to symbolize different elements of the game.

The monsters get a fun and light-hearted party motif. The humans get a mischief motif with references to Bond music and jazz-y elements. The humans also have a triumph motif. Cthulhu has a motif with organs. It also doubles as a ritual motif. Hastur has a hint of a motif with guitars.

Map Songs

Each map gets a different theme during day time. However when it transitions into night time, it goes into the same ambience for the ritual/Cthulhu theme.

Map 1: Mansion - Day Theme Map 2: Carnival - Day Theme Map 3: Mad Mountains - Day Theme Night Theme

Gameplay Songs

There's also a unique party game theme that plays in the game room area of the Mansion map. This song also plays during party game trials. It's a lo-fi song written for the chill vibes of the party games.
