Ritual Night is a multi-player social party game with stealth elements. The players play as a group of thrill-seeking monsters trying to have a good time and complete a yearly ritual to summon the mighty Cthulhu. However, there are humans in disguise among these monsters, and they are here to sabotage...

Ritual Night is coming to Steam & Mobile platforms in Summer 2022.
